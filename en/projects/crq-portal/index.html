PROJECT CASE STUDY

CRQ Solutions Portal & First Responder Automation | Built at Itaú Unibanco

Tech Stack:Datadog WorkflowsServiceNow APIsAWS S3QuickSight dashboardsStackSpot AI APIsGitHub Pages

Business Problem

Scattered SRE operations and lack of standardized triage for critical production incidents in the Lending division, leading to hours of latency for diagnostics and on-call response.

Technical Solution

Developed the CRQ Portal under a static corporate architecture backed by secure S3 proxies and QuickSight analytics. Integrated custom chatbot agents powered by StackSpot API for intelligent infrastructure health-checks and runbook executions. Engineered the 'First Responder' automation workflow using Datadog Workflows integrated with ServiceNow API, scanning log failures and triggering on-call alerts in minutes.

Impact Metrics

Slashed average triage and routing latency of production incidents from hours to under 5 minutes, automating diagnostics runbooks and improving overall squad SRE efficiency by 40%.
