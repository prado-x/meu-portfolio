Foundations & Family

My engineering journey started long before my degree in Information Systems. I was always driven by a deep curiosity about how to optimize software so it would never fail. Outside the office, my family is my ultimate foundation. Happily married, sharing my home with our dogs, and preparing to become a father. This personal drive for stability and extreme ownership is exactly what I bring to every system I architect.

Scale & Core Banking Ecosystem

I have spent the last decade building and scaling automation frameworks and developer tooling in highly regulated financial environments, serving top-tier institutions like Itaú, Bradesco, Santander, Banco Original, Rabobank, Cielo, and BV. I worked hands-on in migrating legacy mainframe systems to modern Java, Python, and TypeScript architectures, establishing comprehensive quality gates (Unit, Integration, E2E, and Chaos) for applications used by millions of users daily.

SRE, Solutions Portal & AI Frontier

In the Lending community at Itaú, I engineered the CRQ Portal—a static GitHub Pages architecture equipped with Stackspot AI chatbots, QuickSight dashboards, and secure admin proxies—centralizing tooling for 850 active repositories. I built the cloud mass data generation platform (Java 21 Virtual Threads, Spring Boot, and ECS Fargate), slashing latency from 5 minutes to 7 seconds. Additionally, I led AI agent discoveries with Devin.AI and Copilot Agent Mode (pushing coverage to 90%) and architected the 'First Responder' SRE automation flow using Datadog and ServiceNow.
