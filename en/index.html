The Vision: Software Engineering in the AI Era

With the rise of Generative AI, code generation speed has increased exponentially. However, the lack of consistency, testing, and robustness has become the single largest bottleneck for modern engineering organizations. Code written at warp speed without proper quality guardrails yields catastrophic technical debt. My vision is to lead the charge in AI-Driven Quality and Spec-Driven Development (SDD). Modern Software Engineering demands a holistic approach: bridging complex Event-Driven architectures and developer platform frameworks with isolated simulation harnesses. My multi-disciplinary background combining Software Engineering, SRE, and Quality Architecture serves as the key differentiator to anticipate production risks, ensure data security, and sustain technical decisions backed by real observability data.
